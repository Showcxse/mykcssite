Lahaina Wildfires Anniversary

Our school community will honor the anniversary of the Lahaina wildfires on Thursday, August 8th. In observance, we encourage all staff and students to wear Lahaina's colors of red or pink. This gesture will symbolize our solidarity and compassion for those affected by the wildfires. We understand that some families may wish to participate in other community events on this day. Students will be excused from school if families choose to attend these activities
