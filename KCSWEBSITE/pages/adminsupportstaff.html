Administration & Support

Michael Stubbs

Head Of School

Originally from Maui, Michael spent over 20 years in New York, earning a BA in Spanish through a Seville, Spain program at Ithaca College. He later obtained an MA in Teaching from Manhattanville College. After teaching diverse subjects for seven years in bilingual schools, he returned to Kihei Charter School in Maui, teaching humanities and Spanish for two years before becoming high school director and later head of school. His focus remains on prioritizing students' well-being while upholding the school's mission.

Cheryl Zarro

Finance Director

Cheryl, a founder of Kihei Charter School, has dedicated over 20 years to its establishment and growth, first as a volunteer and later joining as an employee in 2004. Her entrepreneurial spirit has led her to various business ventures and youth programs, with experience as a business owner and manager. Cheryl remains active in the community, serving on the Board of South Maui Learning ‘Ohana, Inc., the founding organization of Kihei Charter School. She is a proud mother of seven, three KCS alums, and has four grandchildren.

Phill Schmidt

IT Director

Phill adds his expertise in technology and experience, working for KCS since its first year. He has served the technology needs of the school for 23 years. He has multiple Microsoft certifications and Cisco security ratings. A graduate of Texas State University, Phill is dedicated to providing a secure and safe digital environment for all of KCS's students to thrive and reach their potential. His experience in adolescent psychiatric care, sports and school administration make him a high value team member and contributor to the KCS community.

Leslie Baldridge

Elementary School Director

Leslie currently serves as the Director of the Elementary School. She has been with KCS for over 15 years and served in a variety of positions. Mrs. Baldridge is a graduate of California State University, Fullerton with a degree in Child/Adolescent Psychology. Additionally, she holds a graduate degree in Elementary Education and is licensed to teach Elementary and Special Education K-6, with additional certification in grades 5-9 Mathematics.

Pomai Pruett

Middle School Director

Born and raised on Maui, Pomai has been an educator for the past 20 years. She has a degree in United States and European History from the University of Hawaii at Hilo and is certified in elementary education. Pomai is currently the middle school director and has been at KCS for the past 5 years.

Ian Cowan

Middle School Dean

Ian Cowan, also known as Mr. C, is a math teacher and dean of the middle school. He has lived on Maui for 13 years. Upon arriving with his teaching credential, Ian began substitute teaching. After a few years, he started a tutoring company, where he honed his teaching and math skills. He has been teaching at Kihei Charter School for 5 years. Ian has a family that includes a 5-year-old and an 8-month-old. He used to love surfing, hiking, and going to the movies in his free time!

Ellen Federoff

High School Director

Ellen earned her BA from the University of Southern California and a Master’s in Teaching from Loyola Marymount University. Before joining Kihei Charter School, she taught 6th grade for 10 years in Lawndale, California, and served as the Education Outreach Coordinator for the Maui Digital Bus for six years. Credentialed in both Hawaii and California, Ellen, originally from Southern California, played semi-professional volleyball and now races outrigger canoes year-round. She is the Program Manager for Kihei Canoe Club’s Na Opio program and serves on the board of Na Wahine Ho`omana, a women’s support group on Maui.
