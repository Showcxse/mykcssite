About Kihei Charter School

About Us

Everyday, Kihei Charter School is building a stronger, more diverse workforce for tomorrow. Our innovative approach to learning is challenging outdated methods of educating our keiki (children). Through cutting-edge curriculum and game-changing technology, KCS is able to nurture a new generation of engaged learners and chart new horizons in education.

Our Mission

To conceptualize, organize, and build innovative learning environments with custom designed educational programs that will prepare students for a satisfying and productive life in the 21st Century.

Our Vision

To inspire, create and support innovative, cooperative and productive learning environments.

Academic Programs

“We have an innovation addiction… we can’t catch it, but we must continue to chase it.”
- KCS Leadership Team

Elementary School

Students at Kihei Charter Elementary School thrive in a project-based learning environment, engaging in hands-on, real-world challenges that spark creativity. Classrooms foster collaboration with access to technology, while interdisciplinary projects develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and 21st Century skills for future success.

Middle School

At Kihei Charter Middle School, we believe education goes beyond textbooks and tests. We engage students in a dynamic environment focused on project-based and inquiry-based learning. Through hands-on experiences, they solve real-world problems, develop critical thinking, and transition smoothly from elementary to high school.

High School

KCS's High School is a high-performing school, with 65% of graduates pursuing college or trade schools. We prepare students with custom programs, experiential learning, and a dual credit partnership with UH Maui College. With a strong athletics department and a 3,000 sq. ft. fab lab, our students are highly engaged and motivated.

Kihei Charter School has developed innovative academic programs that are centered around choice: individualized learning that is personalized and student-centered. Through cutting-edge curriculum and implementing game-changing technology, KCS is able to nurture a new generation of engaged learners.
